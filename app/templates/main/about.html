About Our Institution

Empowering Kenya's youth through quality technical and vocational education since 2010.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality technical and vocational education that equips students with practical skills and knowledge needed for employment and entrepreneurship in Kenya's growing economy.

Our Vision

To be the leading TVET institution in Kenya, producing skilled professionals who contribute to the nation's development and global competitiveness.

Our History

Established in 2010, our institution has grown from a small technical training center to a comprehensive TVET institution offering a wide range of programs. Over the years, we have:

  • Graduated over 5,000 skilled professionals
  • Established partnerships with 50+ industries
  • Achieved 95% graduate employment rate
  • Expanded to multiple campuses across Kenya
