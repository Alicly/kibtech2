About KITELAKAPEL TECHNICAL TRAINING

KITELAKAPEL TECHNICAL TRAINING is a premier technical training institution committed to providing quality education and practical skills to students. Our institution focuses on equipping students with the necessary technical skills and knowledge required in today's competitive job market.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality technical education and training that empowers students with practical skills and knowledge for employment and entrepreneurship.

Our Vision

To be the leading technical training institution in Kenya, producing skilled professionals who contribute to the nation's development.
